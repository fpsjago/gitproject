From boutique shops to family-owned diners, our local businesses are taking the proper precautions to ensure your next outing is both comfortable and enjoyable. And with venues now open at varying capacity, we’re excited to welcome event planners and event goers alike to rediscover their passion for community.

Below, you will find our event video and guide for more information on what safety measures venues are taking, details about each venue, and social distancing activities you can participate in during your stay.

Ready to Plan Your Outing?

Whether you’re looking to plan an event or attend one, we invite you to explore Clay County, including local shops, restaurants, events, museums, hotels and more.

Clay County has partnered with the Florida Restaurant & Lodging Association and the Clay County Chamber of Commerce to implement their certification programs that make staff and customers aware of a business’ best practices for keeping their stakeholders safe. You’ll see one of these logos with the businesses that earned the certification and are making your safety their priority.
